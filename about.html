About me!

Hello! My name is DragonDreamer! Thank you for visiting my website!

I'm not really sure what My current goals are, but I aspire to one day be a video game developer and have published at least one game! I used to be in college for programming, but am currently not taking any courses. I still hope to graduate one day, if I can go back.

I've always liked imagining worlds and thinking of stories, and video games are the perfect combination of medias to allow oneself to immerse themselves in one. Although, there is a lot that goes into making one, and I don't yet have a lot of the skills needed. I am still hopeful to push through and suceed in bringing my ideas to life one day!

I also sometimes sketch in my spare time! I'm going to be posting it here if I think it's good enough. It's mostly just pencil sketches for now, maybe digital art in the future if I ever invest in a drawing tablet.

I usually play a lot of video games, honestly a bit too much lol. I've been really invested in OverWatch for a long time, but to be honest I might swap to another game soon.

Or maybe I should give up on video games entirely. Would probably be beneficial.
